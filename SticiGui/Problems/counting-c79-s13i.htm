Let's look at a variant of the Monty Hall problem with 100 doors. The doors will be numbered from 1 to 100. Before the show starts, Monty randomly chooses a number n between 1 and 100 and secretly places the car behind door n. Now Christina, a contestant on the show, arrives to play the game. Christina will begin by choosing door 1. Next, Monty secretly chooses a random number from 2..100 (in his head), call it r. If the car was behind door 1, Monty opens every door except for door 1 and door r, showing Christina a goat behind each of those doors. If the car wasn't behind door 1, Monty ignores the random number r he just picked and opens every door except for door 1 and the door with the car, showing Christina a goat behind each of those doors.
