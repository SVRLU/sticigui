Calculating with Probabilities

The California lottery can be played in many different ways, but the simplest version (Daily 3 Straight) works like this. When you buy a lottery ticket, you pick a three-digit number (from 000 to 999). The lottery then randomly draws a random three-digit number, with all possibilities equally likely. If they draw your random number, you win; otherwise, you lose. In practice, the amount you win if your number comes up varies, but for this homework let's assume that you win exactly $550 if your number appears and $0 otherwise.
